Tern — Payments

Service Fee Setup

Connect Stripe to charge clients planning fees and deposit funds directly into your bank account.

📄 Official Tern Help Article ↗
⚠️

Important before you begin This feature is only for planning service fees (e.g. trip planning fees) — not for collecting supplier payments. Only one Stripe account can be linked per Tern account at a time.

💳

Fee overview Tern charges a 0.6% processing fee on top of Stripe's standard fees. Stripe's default credit card rate is 2.9% + 30¢ per charge, plus a 0.4% invoicing fee per paid invoice.

Account Setup

Connecting Your Stripe Account powered by Stripe

1

Open the Service Fees tab on any trip

In Tern, go to any trip you're planning. You'll see a Service Fees tab in the left navigation. If Stripe isn't set up yet, you'll see a prompt to get started.

Click View Settings to continue.

2

Click "Connect Account"

On the Settings page, click Connect Account to launch Stripe's setup flow.

3

Complete Stripe's setup

You'll be redirected to Stripe. Review their Terms & Privacy policy linked in the bottom left.

If you already have a Stripe account, use your existing email and password. Otherwise, create a new account and follow all of Stripe's on-screen instructions carefully.

✅ Make sure your Stripe account is fully verified before proceeding. Incomplete business information or pending verifications can cause delays.
4

Return to Tern — status shows "Pending"

After finishing Stripe's flow, you'll be sent back to your Tern Settings page. Your connection will show as Pending — this is expected.

5

Confirm your email → go live

Check your inbox for a confirmation email from Stripe. Once confirmed, your Stripe account will be Active and your Tern connection will update to Connected.

You're now ready to collect service fees from clients! 🎉

Sending a Fee

How to Invoice a Client

1

Go to Service Fees on the client's trip

Open the trip → click Service Fees in the left nav. You'll now see a prompt to create your first fee.

2

Fill in the invoice details

Select who the fee is for, add a descriptive title the client will recognize, set an amount, and pick a due date.

Click Send Fee Invoice — the client will automatically receive an email from Stripe with the invoice.

3

Track status in Tern

The fee will show as Open in Tern after sending. Use the action menu to manage the fee in Stripe directly, or to grab a payment link to share manually.

Fee Reference

Credit Card Fee Breakdown (Example)

Fee Type Rate Notes
Stripe standard fee 2.9% + 30¢ Per successful charge (domestic credit card)
Stripe invoicing fee 0.4% Per paid invoice (0.5% if upgraded to Invoice Pro)
Tern processing fee 0.6% On top of what Stripe charges
ℹ️

Fees vary by payment method (credit card, ACH/bank transfer, international card). You can control which payment methods clients can use inside your Stripe account under Invoice Settings.

Troubleshooting

Common Stripe Connection Issues

  • Verify your Stripe account is fully validated — incomplete business info (like a missing website) can block connectivity.
  • Confirm all Stripe-required verification steps are completed before connecting to Tern.
  • If issues persist after verifying, disconnect and reconnect your Stripe account from Tern Settings to reset the connection.
✅

After a client pays You'll get an email alert from Stripe, the fee status in Tern will update to Paid, and funds will be deposited directly into your Stripe account for tracking and reporting.
